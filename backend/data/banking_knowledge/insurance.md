# Banking Insurance Products Guide

## Life Insurance

### Term Life Insurance
- Pure protection plan with no maturity benefit
- High coverage at low premium
- Coverage: ₹25 lakh to ₹5 crore
- Premium: ₹500 to ₹2,000 per month for ₹1 crore cover
- Term: 10 to 40 years
- Tax benefit: Premium deductible under Section 80C (up to ₹1.5 lakh)
- Death benefit: Tax-free under Section 10(10D)
- Riders available: Accidental death, critical illness, waiver of premium

### Endowment Plans
- Combination of insurance and savings
- Guaranteed maturity benefit
- Lower returns compared to mutual funds (4-6% historical)
- Premium: Higher than term plans
- Suitable for conservative investors who want guaranteed returns

### ULIPs (Unit Linked Insurance Plans)
- Insurance + investment in equity/debt funds
- Lock-in period: 5 years
- Fund switching allowed (limited free switches per year)
- Charges: Premium allocation, fund management, mortality, policy admin
- Tax benefit: Premium under 80C, maturity under 10(10D) (conditions apply)

## Health Insurance

### Individual Health Insurance
- Coverage for hospitalization, surgeries, and treatments
- Sum insured: ₹3 lakh to ₹1 crore+
- Premium: ₹5,000 to ₹30,000 per year (varies by age and coverage)
- Waiting period: 30 days for general, 2-4 years for pre-existing diseases
- Tax benefit: Premium deductible under Section 80D
  - Self/spouse/children: Up to ₹25,000 (₹50,000 for senior citizens)
  - Parents: Additional ₹25,000 (₹50,000 if senior citizens)

### Family Floater Health Insurance
- Single policy covering entire family
- Shared sum insured among all members
- More economical than individual policies for families
- Coverage: ₹5 lakh to ₹1 crore
- Includes: Hospitalization, day care, ambulance, pre/post hospitalization

### Key Health Insurance Features
- **Cashless Treatment**: Available at network hospitals
- **Pre-existing Disease Coverage**: After waiting period (usually 2-4 years)
- **No Claim Bonus**: 5-50% increase in sum insured for claim-free years
- **Room Rent Limit**: Some policies cap room rent (check before buying)
- **Co-payment**: Percentage of claim amount borne by policyholder
- **Sub-limits**: Caps on specific treatments or procedures
- **Restoration Benefit**: Sum insured restored if exhausted during policy year

### Claim Process
1. Inform insurance company/TPA within 24-48 hours of hospitalization
2. For cashless: Get pre-authorization at network hospital
3. For reimbursement: Pay bills, submit claim form with documents
4. Documents needed: Hospital bills, discharge summary, prescriptions, investigation reports
5. Settlement timeline: 15-30 days after document submission

## Home Insurance
- Covers damage to home structure and contents
- Perils covered: Fire, earthquake, flood, burglary, lightning
- Structure coverage: Based on reconstruction cost
- Contents coverage: Based on replacement value
- Premium: ₹1,000 to ₹10,000 per year
- Add-ons: Terrorism cover, electrical/mechanical breakdown
- Exclusions: Wear and tear, intentional damage, war

## Motor Insurance
### Third-Party Insurance (Mandatory)
- Covers liability for damage to third party
- Mandatory under Motor Vehicles Act
- Premium: Fixed by IRDAI
- Two-wheeler: ~₹500-2,000/year
- Car: ~₹2,500-5,000/year

### Comprehensive Insurance
- Covers own damage + third-party liability
- Includes theft, fire, natural calamities, accidents
- No Claim Bonus: 20-50% discount for claim-free years
- Add-ons: Zero depreciation, engine protect, roadside assistance, return to invoice
- IDV (Insured Declared Value): Market value of vehicle (decreases with age)

## Crop Insurance (PMFBY)
- Pradhan Mantri Fasal Bima Yojana
- Coverage for crop failure due to natural calamities
- Premium: 1.5% for rabi, 2% for kharif, 5% for commercial/horticultural
- Government subsidized premiums
- Covers: Sowing failure, standing crop loss, post-harvest losses
- Available through banks for crop loan borrowers

## Insurance Regulations (IRDAI)
- Insurance Regulatory and Development Authority of India
- Free-look period: 15 days (30 days for online policies) to cancel and get refund
- Grace period for premium payment: 15-30 days
- Claim settlement timeline: 30 days from last document submission
- Grievance redressal: IRDAI IGMS portal (igms.irda.gov.in)
- Policy portability: Can port health insurance between insurers
