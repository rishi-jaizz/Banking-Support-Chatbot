# Loan Products – Comprehensive Guide

## Home Loan

### Overview
A home loan is a secured loan provided by banks and financial institutions for purchasing, constructing, or renovating a residential property. The property itself serves as collateral for the loan.

### Eligibility Criteria
- **Age**: 21 to 65 years (at loan maturity)
- **Income**: Minimum monthly income of ₹25,000 (salaried) or ₹3,00,000 annual income (self-employed)
- **Employment**: Minimum 2 years of work experience for salaried, 3 years for self-employed
- **CIBIL Score**: Minimum 700 (750+ recommended for best rates)
- **Property**: Must be in an approved area with clear title

### Interest Rates
- Floating rate: 8.25% to 9.50% per annum (linked to repo rate)
- Fixed rate: 8.75% to 10.50% per annum (fixed for initial period, then floating)
- Interest rates vary based on loan amount, tenure, and applicant profile
- Women borrowers typically get 0.05% to 0.10% concession

### Loan Amount and Tenure
- **Maximum loan amount**: Up to ₹10 crore (varies by bank and property value)
- **Loan-to-Value (LTV) ratio**: 
  - Up to ₹30 lakh: Maximum 90% of property value
  - ₹30 lakh to ₹75 lakh: Maximum 80%
  - Above ₹75 lakh: Maximum 75%
- **Tenure**: 5 to 30 years
- **EMI**: Calculated using reducing balance method

### Documents Required
1. Identity proof (Aadhaar, PAN, Passport)
2. Address proof
3. Income proof (salary slips for last 6 months, Form 16, ITR for 2-3 years)
4. Bank statements for last 6-12 months
5. Property documents (sale deed, title deed, approved plan)
6. Employment proof
7. Photographs

### Tax Benefits
- **Section 80C**: Deduction up to ₹1.5 lakh on principal repayment
- **Section 24(b)**: Deduction up to ₹2 lakh on interest for self-occupied property
- **Section 80EEA**: Additional ₹1.5 lakh for first-time buyers (for affordable housing)

### Processing Fee
- 0.25% to 1% of loan amount
- Some banks offer zero processing fee during promotional periods
- Legal and technical charges: ₹5,000 to ₹15,000

## Personal Loan

### Overview
An unsecured loan for personal expenses like medical emergencies, weddings, travel, education, or debt consolidation. No collateral required.

### Eligibility
- **Age**: 21 to 60 years
- **Income**: Minimum ₹20,000 per month (salaried), ₹2,40,000 annual (self-employed)
- **CIBIL Score**: Minimum 700
- **Employment**: Minimum 1 year experience with 6 months at current employer

### Interest Rates and Charges
- Interest rate: 10.50% to 24% per annum
- Processing fee: 1% to 3% of loan amount
- Prepayment charges: 2% to 5% of outstanding amount (for fixed rate loans)
- No prepayment charges on floating rate personal loans (as per RBI guidelines)
- Late payment fee: 2% per month on overdue EMI

### Loan Details
- **Amount**: ₹50,000 to ₹40 lakh
- **Tenure**: 12 to 60 months
- **Disbursement**: Within 24 to 72 hours of approval
- **Repayment**: Through EMI via auto-debit from bank account

## Education Loan

### Overview
Designed to finance higher education in India or abroad. Covers tuition fees, living expenses, books, and equipment.

### Eligibility
- Indian citizen with admission to a recognized institution
- Age: 16 to 35 years
- Co-applicant/guarantor required (parent/guardian)
- Good academic record

### Loan Details
- **For studies in India**: Up to ₹10 lakh (no collateral up to ₹7.5 lakh)
- **For studies abroad**: Up to ₹20 lakh (no collateral up to ₹7.5 lakh)
- **Interest rate**: 8% to 15% per annum
- **Repayment**: Starts 1 year after course completion or 6 months after getting a job (whichever is earlier)
- **Moratorium period**: Course period + 1 year

### Tax Benefits
- Section 80E: Full interest deduction for up to 8 years from the year repayment starts
- No limit on the amount of interest deduction

### Covered Expenses
- Tuition and hostel fees
- Examination and library fees
- Books, equipment, and instruments
- Travel expenses (for abroad)
- Caution deposit and building fund
- Insurance premium for the student

## Vehicle Loan (Car Loan)

### Overview
Financing for purchasing new or used vehicles. The vehicle serves as collateral.

### Eligibility
- **Age**: 21 to 65 years
- **Income**: Minimum ₹15,000 per month
- **CIBIL Score**: Minimum 700
- **Employment**: 1 year experience

### Loan Details
- **New car**: Up to 100% of on-road price (up to ₹1 crore)
- **Used car**: Up to 80% of valuation (car should not be older than 8 years)
- **Interest rate**: 7.50% to 13% per annum
- **Tenure**: 1 to 7 years (new car), 1 to 5 years (used car)
- **Processing fee**: 0.5% to 1% of loan amount

### Foreclosure
- No foreclosure charges for floating rate loans
- Fixed rate loans: 2% to 5% foreclosure charges
- Minimum 6-12 EMIs must be paid before foreclosure

## Gold Loan

### Overview
A secured loan against gold jewelry or ornaments. Quick disbursement with minimal documentation.

### Key Features
- **LTV**: Up to 75% of gold value (as per RBI guidelines)
- **Interest rate**: 7% to 17% per annum
- **Tenure**: 3 months to 36 months
- **Amount**: ₹10,000 to ₹1 crore
- **Processing fee**: 0.5% to 1%
- **Disbursement**: Within 30 minutes of verification

### Gold Purity Requirements
- Minimum 18 carat gold accepted
- Gold coins up to 50 grams are eligible
- Ornaments with stones may be accepted but only gold weight is considered
