# Digital Banking & Payments Guide

## UPI (Unified Payments Interface)

### What is UPI?
UPI is a real-time payment system by NPCI enabling instant 24/7 bank transfers via mobile.

### Key Features
- Transaction Limit: Up to ₹1,00,000 per transaction
- Available 24x7x365 including holidays
- Zero charges for customers
- Instant transfer (seconds)
- UPI ID format: username@bankname

### Setup
1. Download UPI app (Google Pay, PhonePe, BHIM, Paytm)
2. Register with mobile number linked to bank account
3. Select bank and link account
4. Set 4/6-digit UPI PIN

### Safety Tips
- Never share UPI PIN
- Verify receiver details before sending
- Don't scan unknown QR codes
- No PIN needed to receive money

## NEFT (National Electronic Funds Transfer)
- Available 24x7x365
- Settlement in half-hourly batches
- No upper limit on amount
- Free for savings account holders (RBI mandate)

## RTGS (Real Time Gross Settlement)
- Available 24x7x365
- Minimum amount: ₹2,00,000
- Real-time settlement within 30 minutes
- Free for savings account holders

## IMPS (Immediate Payment Service)
- Available 24x7x365
- Maximum: ₹5,00,000 per transaction
- Instant transfer
- Charges: ₹2.50 to ₹25

## Internet Banking Features
- Balance and statement viewing
- Fund transfers (NEFT, RTGS, IMPS)
- Bill payments
- FD/RD management
- Loan and credit card management
- Tax payments
- Investment management

### Security
- Two-factor authentication
- Session timeout
- SSL encryption
- Login alerts via SMS/email
- Transaction limits

## Mobile Banking
- Biometric authentication
- QR code scanning
- Cardless ATM withdrawal
- Instant card blocking
- Download only from official app stores

## Cheque Payments
- Bearer, Order, Crossed, Post-dated cheques
- CTS clearing: T+1 business day
- Validity: 3 months from date of issue
- Bounce charges: ₹150 to ₹750
- Bounce is criminal offense under Section 138

## Debit Cards
- RuPay (domestic), Visa, Mastercard (international)
- Free ATM transactions: 3-5/month at other bank ATMs
- Beyond free limit: ₹20/financial, ₹8.50/non-financial transaction
